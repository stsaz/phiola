Commands:

Key     Command
===============
Space   Play/Pause
s       Stop
n       Next track
p       Previous track
Home    First track
End     Last track
[0-9]...  Play track at index

Right   Seek forward
          +Alt   medium step
          +Ctrl  large step
Left    Seek backward

Up      Volume up
Down    Volume down
m       Mute

i       Show tags
r       "Choose random track" setting: on/off
h       Show help
q       Quit
